Improvements

This section presents the improvements that have been made at school in recent years

Improvements made

  • Infrastructure:

    • Renovation of classrooms, roofs, heating or air conditioning systems, and improvements in common spaces such as libraries and sports fields.

  • Technology:

    • Implementation of digital tools to improve learning, such as electronic whiteboards, student devices, or online educational platforms.

  • Educational programs:

    • Expansion of the academic offer with new subjects, language programs, extracurricular workshops, or reinforcement in specific areas such as mathematics or science.

  • Inclusion and accessibility:

    • Improvements in accessibility for students with disabilities, ensuring that everyone has the same opportunities.

  • Security:

    • Increased security measures in the school environment, such as cameras, emergency protocols, and trained personnel.

Pending improvements

  • Infrastructure update:

    • Sometimes older facilities require renovation or expansion. This may include improvement of classrooms, bathrooms, ceilings, lighting, or recreational spaces. Ensuring that all spaces are comfortable, safe and suitable for learning is a constant improvement.

  • Accessibility:

    • There may be areas that are not fully accessible to students with disabilities or special needs. Improving accessibility in terms of ramps, signaling, or adapted pedagogical materials is essential.

  • Educational technology:

    • Although technology is advancing rapidly, it is important to continue incorporating digital tools in the classroom. This includes access to student devices, strengthening Internet connectivity, and using updated digital platforms to teach and learn more interactively.

  • Emotional and psychological support programs:

    • In many schools, especially in times of postpandemia, it is essential to offer more support on an emotional and psychological level. Programs that address students' mental health and offer safe spaces to deal with personal or family problems are a key improvement.

  • Sustainability and environment:

    • Increasing efforts towards sustainability, with recycling projects, energy efficiency, and environmental awareness, can be a priority in many schools. Creating green spaces or school gardens can also be a pending improvement.

  • Continuous teacher training:

    • Although teachers are a key part of the educational process, it is always important that they continue to be trained in new teaching methodologies, technology, and other skills necessary to improve student learning.
